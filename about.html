The Website

The purpose of this website is to provide a central location to access and print models of viruses. While models are optimized for use at Penn States' Maker Commons, all are welcome and encouraged to make use of this website and its contents. All models on this site are often the best 3D represtenations of viruses that can be recreated by 3D printers, and may not be exact replicas. This website is funded and maintained by Virology@PSU, a part of Penn State's Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences.
